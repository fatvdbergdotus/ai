# Intelligent Robot
An intelligent robot is a machine that combines capabilities from Artificial Intelligence, robotics, and sensing technologies to perceive its environment, make decisions, and perform tasks autonomously or with minimal human intervention. Such robots use components like sensors for visual perception, actuators for movement, and algorithms for reasoning, learning, and planning. For example, autonomous robots like Roomba can navigate a home, detect obstacles, and clean floors without direct control, while industrial robots in factories can assemble products with high precision and efficiency. More advanced intelligent robots, such as humanoid robots, can recognize faces, understand speech through natural language processing, and adapt their actions based on experience. These systems demonstrate how intelligent robots integrate perception, learning, and decision-making to operate effectively in complex and dynamic real-world environments.
